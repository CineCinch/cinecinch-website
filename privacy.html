Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

CineCinch is designed to operate entirely on your local device. The application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

CineCinch does not collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Diagnostic data
  • File names, file contents, or metadata
  • System information
  • Network activity or IP addresses
  • Location data

No data is sent to CineCinch servers, third-party analytics services, or any external parties.

Local Processing

All video scanning, analysis, and compression operations occur locally on your computer. No files, processing results, or any other data ever leave your device through CineCinch.

Settings Storage

CineCinch stores your application preferences (such as last scanned folder, output preferences, and UI settings) locally on your device using Windows registry settings. This data never leaves your computer.

Children's Privacy

CineCinch does not knowingly collect any information from anyone. The application is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information to function.
